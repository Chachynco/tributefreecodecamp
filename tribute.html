Taraji P Henson

"Every human walks around with a certain kind of sadness. They may not wear it on their sleeves, buts it's there if you look deep" - Taraji P Henson


Timeline

  • 11 Sep 1970 - Taraji P. Henson was born to Bernice Gordon and Boris Henson in Southeast Washington D.C.
  • 14 May 1995 - Graduated from Howard University where she majored in theatre arts.
  • 29 Jan 2001- Played the role of Yvette, girlfriend of Jody (Tyrese Gibson) in Baby Boy.
  • 22 Jul 2005- Played role of Shug, a pregnant prostitute singing hooks for DJay (Terrance Howard) in Hustle and Flow.
  • 12 Aug 2005- Played role of Camille Mercer, wife of Jeremiah (Andre Benjamin) in Four Brother.
  • 26 Jan 2006- Played role of Shanice Watters, a female contract killer and the partner of Georgia Sykes. (An outspoken feminist and closeted lesbian in Smoking Aces.
  • 29 Jan 2006- Played role of Nedra, a girlfriend of Kenya McQueen (Sanaa Lathan) in Something New.
  • 2007- Played role of Vernell Watson along side Michael Genet and Rick Famuyiwa in Talk to Me.
  • 12 Sep 2008- Played role of Pam Evans, Alice's (Alfre Woodlard) youngest daughter and younger sister of Andrea (Sanaa Lathan) in The Family That Preys
  • 25 Dec 2008- Played role of Queenie, a nurse that finds Benjamin Button (Brad Pitt) and cares for him in The Curious Case of Benjamin Button.
  • 9 Jan 2009- Playes role of Clarice Clark Johnson, married to Dave Johnson (Morris Chestnut) in Not Easily Broken.
  • 9 Feb 2009- Played role of Dana Collins, wife of Coach Al Collins (Forrest Whitaker) in Hurrican Season.
  • 11 Sep 2009- Played role of main character, April, an alcoholic singer that takes care of her sister's children in I Can DO Bad All By Myself.
  • 11 Jun 2010- Played role of Sherry Parker, the overprotective mother of Dre in The Karate Kid.
  • 21 Sep 2011- Played main character, Catana Starks, a coach at TSU. Based on a true story. Starks was the first woman ever to coach a college men's golf team to championship in From the Rough.
  • 2011-2013 She essayed the character of Detective Jocelyn "Joss" Carter in Person of Interest.
  • Jan 7 2015 Till Present Day- Played a main role as Cookie Lyon in the new television musical drama series Empire.
